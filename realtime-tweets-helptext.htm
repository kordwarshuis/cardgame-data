This is the place where real time tweets will stream. All tweets with keyword “bitcoin” will be loaded but which tweets are shown depends on your configuration.

The top of the screen shows the number of incoming tweets every ten seconds.

Also the stream will show handpicked tweets every now and then. It may take a while before a realtime tweet that meets your configuration settings

The default configuration will not show many tweets.

The “clear” button will remove all tweets that are shown and wait for new tweets.

The Tool

See Github repo.

What is it for?

Spread your bitcoin knowledge safely and easily. Recipients might study your gift and then improve their expertise and balance their opinion.

What can you do with the tool?

Share the balanced views supported by recognized experts and optionally add your twist to the message.

The tool consists of a stack of cards. Each card contains a specific misconception or topic. Copy and paste the URL of a card or click on the social media icon. You can smoothly and safely respond to people and offer them verified arguments, regarding the discussed topic. The whole stack of cards is a shareable source of information about Blockchain.
