How does the tool work?

Itâ€™s a game that steadily increases your expertise and knowledge. Likes, reactions, and shares that you receive will result in individual scores. High scores give you increasing recognition in a respected community.

The tool consists of a stack of cards. Each card contains a specific misconception or topic. Copy and paste the URL of a card or click on the social media icon. You can easily and safely respond to people and offer them verified arguments about the topic. The whole stack of cards is a shareable source of information about Bitcoin.
